## What is Scheduly API?

Scheduly API is a REST API built with NestJS that solves a common university problem: finding the best combination of class sections for a semester. Given a list of subjects you need to take, the API evaluates all valid combinations (no time conflicts) and ranks them according to your personal preferences.

The engine works in two phases:

1. **Pruning** — Eliminates sections that violate hard constraints (e.g., avoid a specific professor, protect a lunch break window).
2. **Backtracking + Scoring** — Explores all valid conflict-free combinations and scores each one based on your prioritized rules. Returns the top 3 best schedules.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register and authenticate">
    Create an account and obtain a JWT access token.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your subjects and professors">
    Create the subjects you want to take and the professors who teach them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create sections">
    Add section records for each subject, including start/end times and days of the week.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure optimization rules">
    Define rules like `NO_GAPS`, `PREFER_MORNING`, or `AVOID_PROFESSOR` with priority ordering.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate schedules">
    Call `POST /api/scheduling/generate` with your subject IDs. Get back the top 3 ranked combinations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your favorite">
    Persist the preferred schedule with `POST /api/saved-schedules` for future reference.
  </Step>
</Steps>
